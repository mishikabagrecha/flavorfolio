Recipes

Spaghetti Carbonara

Cook 200g spaghetti in salted water until al dente, then sauté 100g diced pancetta until crispy. Whisk together 2 large eggs and 50g grated Parmesan, then combine with the hot pasta off the heat, adding reserved pasta water for creaminess. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.

Pancake

In a bowl, mix 1 cup flour, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 teaspoon baking powder, and a pinch of salt. In another bowl, whisk together 1 cup milk, 1 large egg, and 2 tablespoons melted butter, then combine with the dry ingredients. Cook on a greased skillet until bubbles form, flip, and serve warm with maple syrup.

Neapolitan Pizza

Make a dough with 2 cups flour, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon yeast, and water, then let it rise for 1-2 hours. Shape the dough, top with 1/2 cup crushed tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil, then drizzle with olive oil. Bake in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for about 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and crispy.

Kaju katli

Grind cashews into a fine powder and mix with sugar and water to form a thick paste. Cook the mixture on low heat until it leaves the sides of the pan, then roll it out on parchment paper. Cut into diamond shapes and garnish with edible silver leaf for a festive touch!

Dosa

Soak rice and urad dal separately for 6-8 hours, then grind them into a smooth batter with water. Ferment the batter overnight until it doubles in size, then add salt. Heat a skillet, pour a ladle of batter, and spread it thin; cook until crispy and golden on both sides!
